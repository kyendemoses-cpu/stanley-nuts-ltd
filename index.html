01
Intake & Grading

All incoming stock is moisture-tested, weighed, and graded on arrival. Sub-standard batches are rejected before entering the facility.

02
Shelling

High-capacity mechanical shellers running at 120 MT/day with automated winnowing to remove shell fragments and foreign matter.

03
Sorting & Cleaning

Optical sorters and vibration tables remove defects, discoloured kernels, and stones to achieve export-grade cleanliness standards.

04
Processing

Kernels are routed to roasting, oil extraction, paste milling, or blanching lines depending on product specification.

05
Packing & Dispatch

Nitrogen-flushed packing lines seal product under modified atmosphere. Full traceability via batch coding to every pallet.

// About Stanley Nuts

BUILT FOR SCALE.

Stanley Nuts Ltd has operated in the African peanut belt since 2017. We began as a regional shelling operation and have grown into one of the continent's highest-capacity peanut processors, serving multinational food manufacturers, commodity traders, and emerging market buyers.

Our 14-hectare production campus houses six processing lines, on-site laboratory facilities, bonded warehousing, and a dedicated export logistics team. We do not compromise on traceability, food safety, or supply reliability.

2017
Founded in Machakos County. First shelling line commissioned at 5 MT/day.
2019
First cold-press oil extraction line installed. Export contracts signed with regional buyers.
2021
ISO 22000 certification achieved. Capacity expanded to 120 MT/day across three product lines.
2023
New optical sorting and paste milling facility opens. Throughput exceeds 85,000 MT annually.
